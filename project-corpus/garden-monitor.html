Process Write‑Up

Early prototypes logged once per minute and chewed through CR2032 cells. I redesigned the duty cycle around a wake‑sample‑transmit‑sleep loop and added ultra‑low power modes on the ESP32 to hit < 30µA deep sleep current.

Architecture

  • Nodes: ESP32 + capacitive soil probe; BLE → gateway
  • Gateway: Solar ESP32 + Wi‑Fi MQTT bridge; local ring buffer
  • Cloud: InfluxDB + retention; Grafana dashboard

Challenges

  • Probe drift in wet soil → implemented auto‑calibration sweeps
  • BLE collisions across 6 nodes → randomized slot scheduling
